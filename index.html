Why this exists

The gospel for a post-truth generation.

We live in a time of austerity, hopelessness, and disconnection. People desire to be seen, known and loved. Jesus is the source of all three. It is not that people are rejecting Jesus but that most have never truly encountered him. These cards exist to change that, one person at a time.

He does not ask you to be whole before you come. He has never once been surprised by your weakness. He knows the way through because He walked it.
